How Dealerships Grow

EVERY dealership has three primary goals:

  • Drive customer traffic
  • Grow ticket averages
  • Increase customer retention

Often, they will work with a different partner to achieve each goal. That’s where the Valvoline™ Advantage becomes a benefit.

Valvoline’s™ array of business solutions, technologies, products, and partner programs allows dealers to transform their business with a single partner.

BUT before we can talk about how Valvoline™ can help them achieve their goals, we have to understand what those are.

BACK

Customer Traffic

Growing their customer base is vital for a dealership’s growth.

An expanding customer base directly impacts sales, leading to an immediate revenue boost. That’s because each new visitor is a potential lead for both immediate and future sales. Increased customer traffic builds a positive brand and reputation. It enhances a dealership's visibility in the community, creates a buzz, and elevates its reputation as a go-to destination for automotive needs.

Valvoline™ can help drive customer traffic with solutions and services like:

  • Customer marketing
  • Sales solutions
  • Service solutions (i.e., Conquest Marketing Services)
BACK

Ticket Averages

By growing ticket averages, dealerships can grow their profit margins and see a better bottom line. As profit margins on new vehicle sales can fluctuate, having a strategy to adapt to market dynamics ensures consistent revenue streams, safeguarding against market volatility.

One of those strategies is for dealerships to offer comprehensive solutions, bundled services, or value-added products, all of which can lead to greater customer satisfaction. This is because customers perceive they are getting more value for their money.

Valvoline™ can help dealerships increase ticket averages with:

  • Business efficiency solutions
  • Service bundles
  • VPS chemicals
  • Incentive programs
  • Sales and technical training
BACK

Customer Retention

Dealerships want to build a strong customer base to better plan for future growth and development. Building this base relies on retaining customers.

A strong customer retention strategy often revolves around building trust and creating loyal customers. Loyal customers can become brand advocates, recommending the dealership to friends and family, thus organically driving more traffic.

This is important because acquiring new customers can be significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones. Also, retained customers often have a higher lifetime value, as they are more likely to return for maintenance services, accessories, and even future vehicle purchases.

Valvoline™ can help dealerships keep customers coming back with programs like:

  • VPS Service Intervals
  • VPS Guarantee
  • Restore and Protect products that keep customers coming back for better engine protection
