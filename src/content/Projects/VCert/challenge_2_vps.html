Benefits to VPS Guarantee

The VPS Limited Lifetime Guarantee allows consumers to obtain additional coverage when their manufacturer’s warranty expires. This is particularly relevant when you consider that the average vehicle on the road is 10 years old and has over 75,000 miles.

Consumers are highly motivated to protect their investments with preventative maintenance services that will help extend the life of their vehicles and provide peace of mind.

BACK

Peace of Mind

The VPS Limited Lifetime Guarantee comes with four levels of coverage, designed for each customer’s vehicle at the time they start the service. For example: a customer with 48,000 miles on their vehicle would receive up to $4,000 worth of coverage on the component serviced for the life of the vehicle.

Valvoline™ Professional Series Services can maximize the life of a vehicle by protecting critical systems like the brakes, cooling system, fuel system, power steering, transmission, and engine. And the Limited Lifetime Guarantee means that protection lasts longer.
