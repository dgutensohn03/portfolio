Why Valvoline

Valvoline™ is an agile company that places a high value on relationships with its partners. Our heavy emphasis on research and innovation forces us to always consider “what’s next” in the industry. Our commitment to service means Valvoline™ is always willing to go the extra mile to deliver hands-on, in-person support to our partners and customers.

A Focus on Innovation

Valvoline™ is a leading innovator, marketer, and supplier of high-performing heavy duty products.

Part of our success stems from our focus on research and development. Valvoline™ is the only engine oil marketer that has its own engine lab. This enables Valvoline™ to self-certify its own products, allowing Valvoline™ to get products into the market faster.

At the New Products Development Lab, at our world headquarters in Lexington, Kentucky, our products are continually tested in normal and abnormal conditions. We do this not to get our products just good enough, but great. We also help our sellers by testing against competitor’s products to compare performance data to validate competitors marketing claims.

A Full Product and Service Portfolio

Valvoline™ isn’t just interested in selling products. We’re interested and invested in the success of the user of those products. It’s why we’ll quickly help troubleshoot and solve problems when equipment issues arise.

This personal touch is unique in an industry that often values quantity over quality. For many of the bigger oil companies heavy duty lubricant sales may be 1 percent of their total revenue. A 10,000-gallon fleet account is very little for them. Not for Valvoline™—we are small enough to care.

Our product portfolio is comprehensively diverse. Not only do we have a unique partnership with Cummins® where they have endorsed and recommended Valvoline™ Premium Blue™ (the ONLY product with such an endorsement), we also have an expansive product line including our Cobalt™ grease and ZEREX™ coolants that have more OEM approvals than any other coolant in the industry. In addition to our relationship with Cummins®, we also work with many other OEMs to optimize the performance of trucks in the on highway and off highway markets.

Technical Support

We don’t just sell oil. Our sales and technical support teams want to help you and your customers succeed. Their focus helps us to hone in on how using the right lubricants can help reduce a customer’s total cost of ownership. They do this with a number of tools and services including:

  • A complete statistical analysis of fleet data
  • Failure component analysis and fleet load analysis
  • Engineering analysis of all information (more than “just send a report”)
  • Determine actionable steps and work together to create implementation and maintenance plans

The Valvoline Technical Services Team is here to support you during the pre-sale process, while onboarding a new customer, and if a customer ever has any fluid-related maintenance issues.
