OEM Programs

Automotive manufacturers create programs for dealers to improve operations, drive up sales, and increase customer loyalty. These programs serve as profit centers for the OEM (original equipment manufacturer). Service and Parts Managers may be particularly faithful to OEM programs because they see a direct financial benefit as well.

Let’s take a look at how the benefits of VALVOLINE™ products and services can help dealerships experience even greater financial and performance success than they find in OEM programs.

Introduction

Here is what’s important to remember about dealership/OEM relationships:

  • Automotive dealerships are partners to OEMs.
  • OEMs rely on the dealer to sell their products to the customer.

An OEM program typically consists of a variety of financial incentives and support services for the dealership. Common elements include:

  • Incentives for bulk oil purchases
  • Customer incentives
  • Training and education
  • Marketing and promotions
  • Warranty and service support

What Did We Learn Today?

OEM programs are oftentimes hard to overcome. By suggesting dealers use Valvoline™ products for non-warranty work or offer VPS services, dealers will still be in compliance with their OEM programs.

With premium products and best-in-class marketing programs and solutions, Valvoline™ can greatly benefit a dealership and their main goals to:

  • Drive customer traffic
  • Grow ticket averages
  • Increase customer retention

While hard to overcome, working through these OEM programs by leading with VPS and non-warranty work can often lead to success for you as a sales rep and for the dealership’s business.
