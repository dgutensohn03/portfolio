Valvoline Scorecard

With the VPS services, promotions, and Limited Lifetime Guarantee, installers can sell more chemical services than ever before. You can illustrate this growth potential to them by using the VPS Scorecard.

It’s important that you work with the installer to populate the Valvoline Scorecard. This makes the installer a part of the process so they become more invested in, and accepting of, the results.

Some of the information you will have to get from your prospective customer includes:

  • Working Days Per Month
  • Average Monthly Repair Orders
  • Current Number of Services per Month per Service
  • Customer Pay Service Orders
  • Chemical Services Sold

A Plan of Action

Each section of the Valvoline Scorecard focuses on a specific area of business. The more information you can add, the easier it will be for an installer to see the potential profit increase they may achieve.

This means you’ll want to complete a detailed profile on the Service Provider (the Start Here - Inputs tab) as this information will inform the Business Impact that VPS Services can have as well as provide insight into an installer’s Profit Potential.

More than just forecasting, the Valvoline Scorecard can be used to create a Plan of Action for both you and the installer.
