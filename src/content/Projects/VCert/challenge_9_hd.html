Cobalt and Cerulean

VALVOLINE™ offers a complete line of greases for a wide range of on- and off-highway heavy duty applications. Two greases in particular stand out: Valvoline™ Cerulean™ and Valvoline™ Cobalt™. In this challenge, we’ll explore what makes each grease unique.

With the ability to provides high temperature and extreme load protection for extended periods of time, Valvoline™ Cerulean™ can help to extend the service life of equipment and lower maintenance costs.
Due to its unique formulation, Valvoline™ Cerulean™ is an ideal product for inventory consolidation.
Valvoline™ Cerulean™ holds up against all top-tier premium heavy duty greases. It provides excellent performance over a variety of operating conditions and may displace other top of the line greases for severe and general purpose lubrication.
As an industry leader, Valvoline™ Cerulean™ has excellent resistance to water wash-out. It also contains oxidation, rust, and corrosion inhibitors for long life performance.
Designed to meet the stressful demands of both industrial and commercial fleet environments, Valvoline™ Cerulean™ is a lithium complex grease.

What Did We Learn Today?

Valvoline™ Cerulean™ and Valvoline™ Cobalt™ are two standout greases for heavy duty applications.

  • Valvoline™ Cobalt™ minimizes wear with its exclusive and proprietary technology. It also protects metal surfaces 8X times longer than other products in the industry. Valvoline™ Cobalt™ grease performs as good, or better, than any other grease when used in severe applications.
  • Valvoline™ Cerulean™ performs effectively in demanding operating conditions, making it an ideal product for inventory consolidation. This lithium complex grease stands up to other premium products in the market.
