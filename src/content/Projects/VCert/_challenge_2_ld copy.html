There are three “players” with VALVOLINE™ DRIVES.

Select eachto learn more.

Consumers

Consumers have a fun way to earn discounts and rewards. DRIVES is the most successful when everyone is involved. This is a program that rewards people for using Valvoline™ products and services and also invites those who aren’t Valvoline™ consumers to give them a try.

Installers

Installers see a boost to their customer base and ultimately to their bottom line. DRIVES presents installers with an opportunity to open up new services to customers who traditionally only came in for “one thing.” And DRIVES increases consumer engagement because they must return to the shop where they were given the game ticket to redeem their prize. DRIVES can even bring in new customers by advertising game codes in local newspapers, on social media, and across other media outlets.

Valvoline™

Valvoline™ manages the complete program. That’s important for the immediate needs of the program and the long-term business of the installer. DRIVES helps installers build and maintain a strong consumer base, so Valvoline™ is able to curate a digital consumer directory, which can be used to build the installer/consumer relationship with relevant communications and promotions.
BACK

Two Paths

DRIVES builds success for installers on two fronts: Driving Traffic and Increasing Retention. The program can be customized in both areas so that it matches the unique goals and needs of the participating installer.

Customer game codes are developed and distributed specifically for an individual installer’s location. And a new code is provided to each customer after every transaction, building excitement and increasing retention.

And, the prizes offered through DRIVES help make it easy for an installer to increase their premium percentage.
