In order for your customer to be comfortable with a product that meets OEM warranties, they will look for a product that is Approved by their manufacturer.

Select each area on the bottle to learn the difference between products that are Approved, Recommended, and Meets.

A = APPROVED

The fluid has undergone extensive manufacturer testing. This involves years of testing that ensures the consumer is provided a genuine product that will perform.

M = MEETS

The fluid has undergone significant required testing to automaker’s fluid specifications.

Valvoline holds itself to high standards. If we claim we meet an OEM spec, we have tested the product and proved that it meets the ENTIRE spec. These specifications are listed for a reason and the consumer needs to know that when we say we meet them, it means all of them. Other products don’t provide the same assurance.

R = RECOMMENDED

The fluid is recommended based on chemistry and industry standard data.
