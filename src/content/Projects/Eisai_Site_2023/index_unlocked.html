Eisai's Annual Compliance Certification

We believe integrity is rooted in all that we do. To demonstrate this commitment, Eisai’s Annual Employee Compliance Certification is an important part of our ongoing efforts as a company.

Compliance at Eisai is owned by all of us. This certification is a useful tool to refamiliarize and reinforce compliance policies that help us conduct business with the upmost integrity. Employees can safely raise any questions or concerns without fear of retaliation.
