Background

An off-highway worksite is a unique work environment. On any given day, work can take place in extreme weather and/or dusty and dirty conditions. Because of this, equipment owners find themselves challenged to maintain their equipment and not lose a day to downtime.

In this challenge, we will look at some of the most common pieces of equipment and the product offerings Valvoline™ has developed to keep this equipment moving.

Excavator

What it is

An excavator is used for digging through earth and rocks. The bucket on an excavator sits on the end of a boom arm. It can be used to transfer material from a jobsite to a dump truck.

  • Average weight: 19–24 tons
  • Average HP: 170
  • Price: $80–500K

Failure points

These machines can accumulate a high amount of on-site hours under a severe duty cycle. The environmental contamination from dirt and dust on-site can significantly increase the wear on an excavator.

Valvoline™ All-Terrain decreases component wear, leading to longer life. It also reduces frictional losses, which can increase fuel economy.

Valvoline™ offers customers Used Oil Analysis. Analyzing used oil provides an evaluation of the lubricant and the equipment. Knowing the conditions of the oil and equipment gives insight into opportunities or threats.

Because many of Valvoline’s™ products have multiple OEM approvals and/or recommendations, they can be used to consolidate inventories.

Digger

What it is

This machine can push materials, dig up earth and rocks, or lift and move construction debris. Because of its versatility, it is often considered the workhorse of a construction site.

Failure points

Because it can perform so many different tasks, it can also be open to a number of different failure points. Heavy loads can strain the system while hydraulic pumps can lose performance because of fluid cleanliness issues.

Select eachto learn more.

Pressure hydraulics require hydraulic oil like Valvoline™ Rust and Oxidation (R&O) oils. They contain a top-tier ashless rust and oxidation inhibition chemistry that is highly suitable for use in hydraulic systems where oils are used for long periods of time.

Pinned joints require a grease that lubricates the pivots under heavy loads. Valvoline™ offers a full line of greases that meet OEM specifications. For equipment that experiences severe loads, Cobalt grease is a great option.

Valvoline™ has a complete Technical Support Team to help customers manage fluid and equipment issues.

Bulldozer

What it is

A bulldozer (sometimes called a loader) is a powerful machine used for pushing earth or rocks. You will often see them being used in the building of roads, farm projects, construction, and demolition.

  • Average weight: 43.5 tons
  • Average HP: 317
  • Price: $250–550K

Failure points

Bulldozers rely on their two tracks to maneuver through a construction site. Additionally, the engine is exposed to high amounts of dirt and dust due to the constant push forward of the bulldozer.

Select eachto learn more.

ZEREX coolant is specifically formulated to meet the demands of heavy duty cooling systems operating in severe service applications.

ZEREX protects all cooling system metals while providing excellent high-temperature aluminum protection.

Valvoline™ Heavy Duty Synthetic Transmission Fluid SAE 50 is selectively engineered for heavy duty manual transmissions operating in severe service applications (temperature extremes, extended drain intervals, critical units, etc.). It is approved against Eaton PS-164 revision 7.

Valvoline™ Palladium™ Moly Grease provides excellent protection against extreme pressures and shock loads by employing high-purity molybdenum disulfide solid lubricant in an alkalinity-balanced thickener system. This grease will meet the Moly requirements of Caterpillar.

Dump Truck

What it is

On nearly every construction site, no matter the size of the project, you will find a dump truck. This is used for removing raw materials from a site and delivering sand, rock, gravel, and dirt back to the site.

While there are many different sizes of dump trucks, they all contain the same basic parts. There is a driver’s cabin at the front of the machine and a bed behind it. Using hydraulic lifts, the bed can be tilted so the materials inside slide out.

Failure points

Dump trucks are constantly moving through a construction site. The engines are running constantly, and they often have to contend with particulate matter from the site.

The hydraulic system relies on careful maintenance and use. Things like overloading the bed can put a severe strain on the system.

Select eachto learn more.

Off-highway equipment relies on the right grease to reduce friction and decrease wear and tear on parts. Valvoline has grease options that can do just that.

ZEREX™ Nitrite Free Extended Life Antifreeze/Coolant has a patented formula that utilizes special inhibitors to protect diesel engines from liner pitting and hard water scale deposits for longer engine life. ZEREX Nitrite Free Extended Life Antifreeze/Coolant uses a patented hybrid organic acid corrosion inhibitor package to protect cylinder liners from cavitation, liner pitting, and rust and corrosion in your engine.

It meets CAT EC-1 and Cummins 3666286 specifications.

Concrete Mixer

What it is

A concrete mixer combines aggregate, cement, sand, or gravel with water to form concrete. It does this with the use of a revolving drum. The mixing can occur during transport or concrete can be loaded from a plant, maintaining a liquid state while turning the drum.

Failure points

When these machines are under-greased, over-greased, or using the wrong grease for the application, their parts can wear faster, undergo seizures, or cause the equipment to experience a mid-job breakdown.

Select eachto learn more.

Using the right grease reduces downtime in off-highway equipment. Since off-highway equipment owners don’t have spare machines on-site, they need their equipment to be working at all times.

Grease is needed for maximum performance and reduced maintenance.

Concrete mixers often will experience failed tub rollers. Using Cobalt grease will reduce these failures, minimizing downtime and repair costs for your customers.
