A History of Innovation

Valvoline™ has remained relevant since its founding in 1866 because it never stopped innovating. For over 150 years, our scientists and technicians have worked to develop fluids and lubricants that meet the needs of engines today with an eye towards tomorrow.

We do this because when it comes to a consumer’s vehicle, we believe that their problem is our problem too. This is why we look back on the past in order to create innovative, quality products to help them reduce their total cost of ownership (TCO) and help their engine reach its peak performance levels.

Tools for Success

In addition to providing quality products and best-in-class services, Valvoline™ has developed a number of marketing and sales tools to help installers build the success of their business.

Select eachto learn more.

DASH – DASH is an online portal for installers and dealerships to access the specific tools available to them. DASH helps make Valvoline™ easy to do business with.
Valvoline™ University – Valvoline™ University is an online platform that provides Valvoline™ installers with classes on products and services along with sales techniques. Market data has shown when installer employees are informed about products and marketing programs, premium sales have increased by 20%, and customers spend 8% more overall on each visit.
Marketing Support Programs – DRIVES, SPARK, Reward Program, and other Valvoline™ fully-funded and managed marketing programs help drive new business and help retain customers.

Valvoline Scorecard

Potential customers like to hear that Valvoline™ can help their business, but they like to see the financial potential even more. The Valvoline™ Scorecard can be used to show how, in their specific business setting, a customer could increase consumer retention and, ultimately, sales.

The scorecard will help you to do that by consolidating your ideas, the installers needs, their current sales conditions, and market data.

It will also help to visualize the average potential gross profit per gallon they could realize based on the programs you recommended. For some customers, the numbers realized here can help make up the pricing difference between Valvoline and a competitor.
