A History of Innovation

Over the years, Valvoline™ has been innovative by:

  • Creating the first engine oil
  • Formulating the first racing oil
  • Created the first synthetic oil
  • Creating the first high-mileage motor oil

As you can see, for over 150 years Valvoline™ has been a leading innovator in engine oil lubrication. Our automotive experts are always innovating and pushing boundaries, preparing our partners for the future, and offering products and recommendations to service the next generation of vehicles.
